Šaltibarščiai

Description

This Lithuanian family recipe for šaltibarščiai is from the old country. It makes an awesome summer soup! Serve with warm Yukon Gold potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • 1 pound beets, peeled and shredded
  • 1 large English cucumber - peeled, quartered, and sliced
  • 1 bunch fresh dill, minced
  • ¼ cup minced chives

Steps

  1. Place eggs into a saucepan in a single layer and cover with water by 1 inch.
  2. Cover the saucepan and bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from the heat and let eggs stand in hot water for 15 minutes.
  4. Drain, then run under cold water to cool.
  5. Peel and chop eggs.
  6. Make the sauce
  7. Place into a large bowl. Add buttermilk, beets, cucumber, dill, and chives.
  8. Stir gently to combine.
  9. Chill in the refrigerator for 24 hours before serving.
